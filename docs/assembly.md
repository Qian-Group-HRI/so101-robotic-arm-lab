## Follower Arm Assembly (SO101 / SO100)

> The follower assembly is almost identical to the leader. The main difference is at the **end-effector** (gripper vs handle), and the power supply used (5 V vs 12 V depending on kit version).

                    <li>Attach the shoulder part.</li>
                    <li>Tighten the shoulder part with 4 M3x6mm screws on top and 4 M3x6mm screws on the bottom</li>

## Leader Arm Assembly (SO101 / SO100)

> The steps are analogous to SO100, but make sure you match the updated SO101 gear ratios.

<table align="center" style="width:100%">
    <tbody>
        <tr>
            <td>
                <div align="center"><code>Gripper/Handle</code></div><br>
                <ul>
                    <li>Mount the leader holder onto the wrist and secure it with 4 M3x6mm screws.</li>
                    <li>Attach the handle to motor 5 using 1 M2x6mm screw.</li>
                    <li>Insert the gripper motor, secure it with 2 M2x6mm screws on 
                    each side, attach a motor horn using a M3x6mm horn screw.</li>
                    <li>Attach the follower trigger with 4 M3x6mm screws.</li>
                </ul>
            </td>
